Giving college students the space for their business.

Founded in 2025 by Elicia Powell, our crochet shop is dedicated to celebrating the beauty and creativity of handmade crafts. What started as a dorm-room project has grown into a brand recognized for warm designs, quality materials, and accessible products. Our mission? Create pieces that make people smile, last for years, and support makers everywhere.

We believe that handmade means more than craft—it’s about connection, care, and empowering others. As a team of friends (and students!), we work together to bring authentic, playful, and imaginative crochet products to our community.

Our promise stays the same: unique products, thoughtful design, and making every customer feel welcomed and special every day.

OUR JOURNEY
It all began in Minneapolis, helping friends and classmates discover their love for handmade crafts...
OUR MISSION
To spread warmth and joy through handmade crochet products.
